Week 3 Project

Intel Sustainability Timeline

A clean, responsive timeline showing key sustainability milestones. Hover a milestone to reveal details, on mobile tap and scroll.

Timeline Overview

Desktop: horizontal timeline with hover details. Mobile: stacked cards for easy reading.

2019

RISE Strategy

A framework for responsible, inclusive, sustainable, enabling actions.

RISE organizes goals into measurable programs and long term targets.

2020

Renewable Energy

Scaling renewable electricity to reduce operational emissions.

Progress depends on efficiency, sourcing, and reliable grid partnerships.

2021

Water Stewardship

Conservation and watershed restoration as local impact work.

Projects focus on conservation, recycling, and restoration partnerships.

2022

Net Zero Path

Commitment toward net zero for Scope 1 and 2 by 2040.

Combines clean power, process improvements, and accountable reporting.

2023

Circular Economy

Reduce waste and keep materials in use longer through reuse and recycling.

Circular programs reduce landfill and improve material recovery rates.

2030

Scaling Targets

Long term goals expand across operations and supply chain impact.

Measurement, incentives, and execution are what make targets real.

How it works

  • Hover a milestone to reveal details.
  • Keyboard: Tab to focus a card and reveal details.
  • Mobile: cards stack vertically for readability.
